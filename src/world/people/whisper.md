# {{ title }}

Long grey hair with a scruffy, messy beard.

Whisper, a rough man aged beyoond his years, is the head of the [[LoFera]] branch of the [[Hollow Keys]] and has been for more than two decades. Few know who he was before this, though. Even fewer know the name given to him at birth.

Typically a man of few words, he has spent his years managing and caring for the members within his branch of the [[Hollow Keys]]. But, in recent days, contracts have come in which have been against the tenets of the Hollow Keys. Whisper slowly began to realize this and, after looking into a contract which was only just completed by [[Pennyglass]], he had confirmation.

Whisper began denying these contracts coming in...

> "... Hollow Keys should never be involved directly in these politics... However dark a guild it may seem, we are labour with a purpose. And, until this bullshit, we remained free of the knowledge and responsibility of **why**... But now, we have become soldiers in an invisible war that we did not fucking sign up for... Shit... I'm empty..."

Soon after he began to deny these contracts, he was pressured by the new leadership which had seemed to have taken hold of the Hollow Keys. Whisper stood his ground, though, and has been paranoid of the possible retaliation that could be headed his way...
