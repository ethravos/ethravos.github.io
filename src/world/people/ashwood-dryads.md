Also known as the "Ashwood Grove" or "Wardens of the Feylume".

## Description

The grove seems to almost rest in an invisible plate that the forest around yields to. Vines and long tendrals of twisted stone and wood construct nearly every structure. Leaves and trailing plants drape chaotically over nearly every surface, becoming especially thick aroun the doorways leading into most structures.

There is a large, damaged, but very alive, gnarled oak tree toward the center of the mostly circular plot of land.

Birds, animals, flora, and fauna of all kinds weave through the glade as though it is still a part of the surrounding woodland. Crystal lights within bulbous wooden lamps shine and illuminate the village in a soft, warm orange light.

## History

The Ashwood Dryads have existed for over 300 years.

They have a rich culture and are a community that has maintained the [[Feylume Forest]] since their formation. They have no documentation of the exact circumstances of how they were formed, but they know it was formed by a couple known as the [[Thurvanar]], or "children of the forest. Specifically the male, Sylvarax-Thuranar and the female, Kethryl-Thurvanar.

## Current Day

Led by a council known as the "Riall", the Ashwood Dryads maintain and protect the [[Feylume Forest]].

The grove is bright as ever, now that some time has passed since Altiath's death. Teachings of his specific kind have also ended in favor of a much more traditional instruction, provided by Yaamtec-Trouskan.

New projects have been sparked in attempts to recover the local bat population as recently they have seemed to migrate further East. There has also been a rapid resurgance in the interest of minor Wild Shape practice and herbalism as a combination of "Elven Willow", "Barberry", and "Maruera"
