# {{ title }}

Before the [[First Dawn]], the Feywild was originally a simple island chain, known as the [[Feywild|The Feyland Isles]] within the [[Elrose Sea]].

During "some kind of parting" of the Old and New Gods, the Feyland Isles were stretched into a seemingly infinite series of forest continents.
