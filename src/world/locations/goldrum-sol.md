# {{ title }}

There is a story told today about the origin of this city within such a unique, fissured scape. Long before [The Benefaction](/world/events/the-benefaction), during an intense battle between [Ven](/world/gods/ven) and [Iso](/world/gods/iso), the [Goldrum Mount](../goldrum-mount) within the [White Crown Sierra](/world/locations/white-crown-sierra) was split down the middle.

Soon after [The Benefaction](/world/events/the-benefaction), the chasm was used as a means of traveling quickly through the Crown Peaks. Early historical records suggest that it was often used as an outpost used by bandits to rob those walking through.
