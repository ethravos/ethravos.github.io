Goldrum Sol is an ancient city within the split mountain called the [[Goldrum Mount]].

The city is lively and extremely dense with platforms and structures weaving in and out of the sides of the mountain. A wide, flat road runs straight through the entire city from East to West, acting as a main avenue for the people and traders traveling through.

There are small elevator mechanisms for transporting things vertically to the buildings or platforms above.

## The Goldrum Sol Library

The library within Goldrum Sol is one of the largest collections of knowledge in all of [Ethravos](/). It was consstructed by the [Delvan Academy](#the-delvan-academy) over four centuries ago. They also currently maintain and use the library as a core component to their campus.

## History

The story told about the origin of this fissured scape is that long before [[The Benefaction]], during an intense battle between [[Ven]] and [[Iso]], the [[Goldrum Mount]] within the [[White Crown Peaks]] was split down the middle.

Soon after [[The Benefaction]], the chasm was used as a means of traveling quickly through the Crown Peaks. Early historical records suggest that it was often used as an outpost used by bandits to rob those walking through.
