# {{ title }}

The story told about the origin of this fissured scape is that long before [The Benefaction](#), during an intense battle between [Ven](#) and [Iso](#), the [Goldrum Mount](../goldrum-mount) within the [White Crown Peaks] was split down the middle.

Soon after [The Benefaction](#), the chasm was used as a means of traveling quickly through the Crown Peaks. Early historical records suggest that it was often used as an outpost used by bandits to rob those walking through.
