    Veyra Sable

    Assissin within the Hollow Keys.

    She was hired to assassinate Altiath-Drelkin.

    She has been in the LoFera branch of the Hollow Keys for about 12 years. 9 years ago, when Whisper took over the LoFera branch, he took fewer and fewer contracts that suited her skillset.

    3 years ago, she contacted the Master Key to request new leadership. And, although the request was denied, they did promise to investigate and bring about change in which commissions were accepted.

    The jobs only really began to increase about a year and a half later.

    A month ago, Veyra was given a contract for the assassination of Altiath-Drelkin. Unfortunately, unlike what is standard for all Hollow Key contracts, she was told that the reason was for the purpose of haulting his studies and progress into entering the Feywild.
