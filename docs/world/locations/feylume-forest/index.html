    The Feylume Forest

    The forest that swallows fates

    The Feylume Forest is a deep, mysterious, and ancient land.

    Some say the forest itself alters to be larger or smaller, almost as though it breathes through space and time itself.

    Long before the Second Razing, The Feylume Forest is believed to have originally been a part of the Feywild.

    It is also the home to LoFera.
