Feylume Forest

The forest that swallows fates

  • Deep and mysterious
  • Some say the forest itself alters to be larger or shorter, almost as though it breathes in and out space and time itself
  • Long before the Second Razing, The Feylume Forest is believed to have originally been a part of the Feywild.
  • Home to LoFera, a city a few miles into the forest
