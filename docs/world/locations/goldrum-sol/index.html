Goldrum Sol

There is a story told today about the origin of this city within such a unique, fissured scape. Long before The Benefaction, during an intense battle between Ven and Iso, the Goldrum Mount within the White Crown Sierra was split down the middle.

Soon after The Benefaction, the chasm was used as a means of traveling quickly through the Crown Peaks. Early historical records suggest that it was often used as an outpost used by bandits to rob those walking through.
