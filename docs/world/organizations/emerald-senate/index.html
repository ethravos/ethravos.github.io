    The Emerald Senate

    Also known as The Senate Of The Emerald Petal.

    Up until Aurora went to her mothers old friend to ask about the book, The Emerald Senate was unaware that Aurora existed.
    Once they learned of her existence, they dispatched three bountyhunters to take her captive (or kill her if necessary).
    They lost her on her way into LoFera.
    These assassins then found her, Verdant, and Pennyglass in the Stones Throw Tavern and attacked.

    The names of the assassins was:

    Elf man : Elarion Duskweaver
    Human woman : Nyx Corven
    Dwarvish woman : Hilda Flintvein
